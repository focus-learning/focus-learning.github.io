Our Mission

We aim to provide free resources to help students enrich their mathematical ability. We offer livestreams, powerpoints, handouts, and much more!

Our Resources

Currently, Focus Learning offers livestreams, handouts, and free discussion.

  • For livestreams, please check the schedule in the Common Core tab. All our livestreams will be done on our Twitch account, which can be found in the link as well. After each livestream, the video will be posted on our YouTube channel and the powerpoint will be posted on our website.
  • Handouts will be posted in the Handouts tab.
  • Join the discussion on our Discord server. This is the easiest way to ask questions and discuss math with other people.
